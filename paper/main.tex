\begin{abstract}
We study operator learning for initial-value problems of delay differential equations (DDEs)
using Fourier Neural Operators (FNOs). We benchmark on several families of DDEs with discrete
and distributed delays, generate paired training data using high-accuracy solvers, and train a
baseline FNO model to learn the solution operator mapping history functions and parameters to
future trajectories.
\end{abstract}
